Community Bulletin

The annual community festival was celebrated with great enthusiasm. Residents participated in cultural programs, food stalls, games, and awareness campaigns. Volunteers helped organize activities for children and senior citizens. The event encouraged unity, teamwork, and social interaction among community members. Local artists and performers showcased their talents during the evening celebration. Many visitors appreciated the clean environment and security arrangements made by the organizing committee.
